Key Context — March 25
Food vs Fuel lag: Fuel prices pass through to food in 4–8 weeks. The second-wave food shock from fertiliser disruption will peak April–June.

Fertiliser alert: Urea +30–40%. Gulf states supply 49% of world urea. Spring planting season starting in Northern Hemisphere — timing is critical.

Ceasefire effect: Today's Brent drop (–5.8%) will take 2–4 weeks to reach pump prices, and 6–10 weeks to reach food. If talks succeed, April food prices may be the last high reading.
